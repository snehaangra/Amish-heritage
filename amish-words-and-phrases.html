Amish Words and Phrases

The Amish speak a version of German that is called Pennsylvania Dutch. Some Amish people also speak a variation of the Swiss and English. Amish communities use different dialects and phrases by region.

Here are some common words and phrases listed in alphabetical order.

  • ach - oh
  • ach jah - oh yes
  • aendi - aunt
  • appeditlich - delicious
  • ault - old
  • awlee eppa - everyone
  • blume - flowers
  • brode - bread
  • bruder - brother
  • buwe - boy
  • dawg - day
  • dat - dad
  • deerich - silly, ridiculous, foolish
  • denki - thank you
  • dochder - daughter
  • fraa - wife
  • gaarde - garden
  • gaul - horse
  • Gotte - God
  • gute - good
  • haus - horse
  • hochmut - pride
  • jah - yes
  • kault - cold
  • kind - child/kid
